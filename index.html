Investment Thesis

Value flows to whoever creates order from chaos.

Life has been doing this for 3.8 billion years. AI is now accelerating it. Three things enable this organization: energy, materials, and information. They've been accelerating together throughout history — and we are entering the most rapid phase.

The fund is not static. It evolves.

Materials equilibrate over time — high prices attract new supply. But information compounds — network effects at near-zero marginal cost. So we hold materials tactically while supply is constrained, and rotate into compounding positions as cycles mature.
